About Me

Hello! I'm Sokcheat, an instructor at STAD specializing in Frontend Development, Blockchain Technology, and Data Analytics. With a passion for teaching and technology, I've created these HTML Mastery Challenges to help students develop genuine coding skills without relying on AI tools or search engines.

Throughout my career, I've observed that students who learn to solve problems independently develop stronger foundational skills. These exercises are designed to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills that are essential for any web developer.

My teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning and practical application. I believe that the best way to learn is by doing, and these challenges provide a structured way to practice HTML skills in meaningful contexts.

Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching web development

Learn by Doing

Practical exercises that require active problem-solving create deeper understanding than passive learning.

Embrace Constraints

Limitations foster creativity and force you to truly understand the tools at your disposal.

Build from Fundamentals

Mastering the basics creates a strong foundation for more advanced concepts in frontend, blockchain, and data analytics.

Encourage Exploration

The best learning happens when students discover solutions through experimentation and critical thinking.
